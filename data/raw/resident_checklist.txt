Dutch Resident Income Tax Filing Document Checklist:

Gather the following documents and information before starting your annual tax return (P-form) on the Mijn Belastingdienst portal:

1. Personal Information:
- DigiD: Secure login details for yourself and your fiscal partner.
- Citizen Service Number (BSN): Required for you, your tax partner, and any dependent children.
- Bank Account (IBAN): Needed for tax refunds or direct debit payments.

2. Income Documentation:
- Annual Income Statement (Jaaropgave): Provided by your employers for all jobs held during the tax year.
- Alimony/Spousal Support: Documentation of any support payments received from an ex-partner.
- Other Income: Details of business income, freelancing, or pension benefits.

3. Housing and Real Estate (Primary Residence):
- WOZ-waarde (WOZ value): The municipal valuation of your primary residence, determined on January 1st of the previous year (shown on your annual municipal tax bill).
- Mortgage Statement (Jaaropgave Hypotheek): Detailing the outstanding mortgage balance and interest paid during the tax year.
- Notary Settlement Sheet (Notarisafrekening): If you bought or sold your home during the tax year.

4. Savings and Investments (Box 3 Assets):
- Bank Statements: Showing bank and savings account balances (domestic and foreign) as of January 1st.
- Investment Portfolios: Annual statements showing values of stocks, bonds, and mutual funds as of January 1st.
- Cryptocurrencies: Value of all crypto assets held on January 1st.
- Debts and Loans: Personal loans or other debts that can be deducted from your Box 3 taxable assets.

5. Deductible Expenses (Keep proof of payments):
- Medical Expenses: Out-of-pocket healthcare costs not covered by compulsory or voluntary health insurance (excluding deductible excess/eigen risico).
- Charitable Donations: Donations made to approved cultural or general public benefit organizations (ANBI).
- Spousal Support Paid: Payments made to a former spouse.
- Private Pension Premiums: Premium payments for annuities (lijfrente) or disability insurance (AOV).
